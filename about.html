Who We Are

We Don't Just Teach Subjects.
We Build Confidence.

Most students don't fail because they're not smart enough — they fail because they never had access to the right teaching. That's the gap Ahmed's Institute was built to close.

Founded in 2015 by Er. Salman Ahmed, a Government Polytechnic + Osmania University graduate, AIE started with a simple belief: every student deserves a teacher who actually cares about their success. Today, 1,500+ students later, we've helped clear over 8,000 subjects — many of them backlogs and supply exams that students thought were impossible.

Our Journey

From a Small Classroom
to 1,500+ Success Stories

2015

The Beginning

Er. Salman Ahmed starts Ahmed's Institute of Excellence in Azampura, Hyderabad with a handful of diploma engineering students and one mission: make learning accessible.

2017

Expanded to B.E/B.Tech

Word-of-mouth results bring in engineering degree students. Added all major branches — Civil, CSE, Mechanical, EEE, ECE. Faculty team grows.

2019

1,000 Subjects Cleared

Milestone: students coached at AIE clear 1,000+ supply and regular subjects across JNTU, OU, and SBTET universities. Intermediate and school coaching added.

2022

Vocational & Online Courses

Launched practical skill courses — AutoCAD, Python, Web Development, E-TABS — plus online coaching platform for students who can't attend in person.

2025 & Beyond

8,000+ Subjects & Growing

1,500+ students trained, 550+ graduates produced, 13+ faculty members, 4.9★ Google rating — and we're just getting started. Entrance exam coaching (E-CET, EAMCET, POLYCET) now in full swing.
