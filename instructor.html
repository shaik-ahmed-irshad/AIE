Founder & Chairman

Er. Salman Ahmed

Er. Salman Ahmed is the visionary founder and chairman of Ahmed's Institute of Excellence. A qualified engineer himself, he established the institute with a single mission: make quality engineering education accessible and affordable for every student in Hyderabad.

Having observed first-hand how many talented students drop out or fail due to lack of proper guidance and financial constraints, Er. Salman created a teaching model that combines concept-first pedagogy with genuinely low fees — starting at just ₹2,000 per subject.

Under his leadership, Ahmed's Institute has grown from a small tuition centre in 2015 to a recognized coaching hub that has helped 8,000+ students clear their subjects across Engineering, Diploma, Intermediate, and School levels.
